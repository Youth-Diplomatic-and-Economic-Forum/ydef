Young Diplomatic and Economic Forum

India' s first diplomatic and economic forum by the youth, for the youth

A 2-day mega event, to be hosted in New Delhi in the 1st week of July; The 4th quarter, aims to be a pioneer. Pioneer, in terms of being the first pan-India diplomatic event hosted by high schoolers. BrianX event would be where famous social media celebrities, parliamentarians, professors and diplomats engage in 1 on 1 conversations with the motto “The final quarter of India's 1st century as a free nation”.

Our Aim

To pioneer a platform where high school, college students and academicians across India engage in meaningful diplomatic dialogues with renowned figures in international politics, fostering a deeper understanding of global issues and smart diplomacy.

Facilitate discussions on India's economic landscape, focusing on reforms, job creation, the creator economy, and emerging technologies like lithium-ion batteries and cryptocurrency, aiming to inform and inspire youth about economic prospects.

To broadcast these insightful conversations worldwide, promoting global awareness of India's perspectives on international politics, economic policies, and regional responsibilities, thereby enhancing India's diplomatic outreach and global influence.

About Us

The Youth Diplomatic and Economic Forum (YDEF) is a dynamic platform that brings together young leaders, diplomats, and experts from around the world. Their mission is to foster dialogue, exchange ideas, and address pressing global issues related to diplomacy, international relations, and economic development. YDEF aims to empower the next generation of change-makers by providing them with practical knowledge, networking opportunities, and exposure to influential voices in the field.

Our Vision

The Youth Diplomatic and Economic Forum envisions a world where young leaders actively shape global affairs. We believe in empowering the next generation to engage in meaningful dialogue, bridge cultural divides, and contribute to positive change
